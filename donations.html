All About Donations :


If you want to help the needy ones , you can donate us by clicking the Help Poor option at the bottom of this page

Here we have two categories available
1. You can pay the Default amount(Rs.500) as set by our system ,by clicking on the Pay Default Amount option
2. You can pay the Desired Amount (whatever you want),by clicking on the Pay Desired Amount option

Help Poor Policy:
We value your vision towards the needy section of the society and we value your donations too,so we process your Donations in the safest way so that your Donations reach the right place at right time. For this we directly accepts your payments as donations where there is no third party involbved in between you and us , which minimises the risk of mishandling of the resources and opens doors of happiness for the needy ones. The Gyani Wisdom HELP POOR initiative is to help the needy ones in our surroundings by providing them food, clothes , education and other basic necessities of life to make them aware of the Society. Now you will think that how we process your Donations, so the answer is quite simple... As told earlier we have no third party involved between you and us, so you can be carefree of your donations. secondly we look around for the real needy ones and hard workers in the surroundings (not the fake ones who opts dark ways to earn his /her living even though he/she is poor)After identifying the true person in need your donations are then used up to provide him / her food , clothes , medicines and other needs of him.
At last the Gyani Wisdom Help Poor Team Thanks you all for all your support and help which you have provided to help and uplift the such section of the society.
